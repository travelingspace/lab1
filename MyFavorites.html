Favorite Movie

Pump Up the Volume

This movie stars a young Christian Slater playing a teenage pirate radio DJ, before the internet age. The soundtrack is amazing and the content is thoughtful, provoking, and still rings true to the struggles of today that we face as a culture and in our formative years as human beings.
